I offer a full range of services to take your business online and keep it running. Every service is managed end-to-end — you don't need to touch a terminal, configure a server, or deal with DNS records. I handle it all.

Services are grouped into three categories: **Infrastructure**, **Development**, and **Growth**.

---

## Infrastructure

The foundation. These services keep your site online, your domain pointing to the right place, and your email working.

### Hosting & Uptime Management

I provision and configure your hosting environment, monitor uptime around the clock, and respond to incidents. If something goes down, I'm on it before you notice. This includes server configuration, performance tuning, and environment management.

### Domain & DNS Management

I handle domain registration, DNS configuration, SSL certificate provisioning, and annual renewals. You pick the domain name — I take care of the rest, including propagation, CNAME/A records, and any third-party DNS integrations you need.

### Business Email

Professional email under your own domain. I set up and administer Google Workspace or a custom SMTP provider, configure SPF/DKIM/DMARC records for deliverability, and handle ongoing account management so your email just works.

### Database Management

For projects that need persistent data, I provision and manage a Neon PostgreSQL database — including schema setup, automated backups, connection pooling, and monitoring. You get a production-grade database without worrying about infrastructure.

## Development

The build. These services cover everything from a marketing site to a full custom web application.

### Website Design & Development

I design and build multi-page websites from customizable templates, fully tailored to your brand. The base package includes up to 5 pages — landing page, about, services, contact, and one more of your choice. Every page is responsive, fast, and production-ready.

### Web App Development

For businesses that need more than a static site. I build custom dashboards, client portals, and SaaS tools with authentication, role-based access, CRUD operations, and third-party integrations. This is full-stack development — frontend, backend, API, and database — delivered as a single managed product.

### Contact Form & Email Automation

A fully functional contact form wired to your inbox with email forwarding, auto-responders, and spam protection. Visitors fill out the form, you get the email, and they get a confirmation — no manual steps, no missed leads.

### Ongoing Maintenance

Websites need upkeep. I handle regular updates, dependency patches, content changes, and bug fixes on a recurring basis. This keeps your site secure, performant, and up-to-date without you lifting a finger.

## Growth

The next step. These services help you get found, understand your traffic, and get help when you need it.

### SEO Setup & Optimization

I set up technical SEO from the ground up: metadata, Open Graph tags, sitemaps, structured data (JSON-LD), Core Web Vitals optimization, and Google Search Console integration. This gives search engines everything they need to index and rank your pages correctly.

### Analytics Integration

I integrate Google Analytics 4 or a privacy-first alternative like Plausible, configured with goal tracking and event reporting. You get a clear picture of who's visiting, what they're doing, and where they're coming from — without guesswork.

### Priority Technical Support

Dedicated support via email with guaranteed response times. If something breaks, needs changing, or you just have a question — you get a fast, direct answer from the person who built your product. No ticket queues, no bots.
